⚡ FBGM Quick Export

One-click export of your FBGM league data into the Advisor.

Option 1: Direct Transfer (recommended)

Opens the Advisor and sends your data directly — no file download needed.

1 Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

⚡ Open in Advisor

Tip: If the bookmarks bar isn't visible: Ctrl+Shift+B (Chrome/Edge) or Ctrl+B (Firefox)
Option 2: Download JSON

Downloads a JSON file that you can upload manually in the Advisor.

1 Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

📁 Download JSON

Usage

1 Open your league in Football GM (play.zengm.com/l/9/...)
2 Click ⚡ Open in Advisor — the Advisor opens with your data loaded instantly
✅ What gets exported: Players, Teams (with Seasons/Standings), GameAttributes, Draft Picks — everything the Advisor needs.
⚠️ Important: The bookmarklet only works on the FBGM page (play.zengm.com or play.football-gm.com). It only reads data — it does not modify your league. Your browser may block the popup the first time — allow popups for the site and try again.
