⚡ FBGM Quick Export

One-click export of your FBGM league data — skip the manual Tools → Export League workflow.

Setup (one time only)

1 Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

⚡ FBGM Quick Export

Tip: If the bookmarks bar isn't visible: Ctrl+Shift+B (Chrome/Edge) or Ctrl+B (Firefox)
2 Done! The bookmarklet is installed.

Usage

1 Open your league in Football GM (play.zengm.com/l/9/...)
2 Click ⚡ FBGM Quick Export in your bookmarks bar
3 The JSON file downloads automatically — upload it in the FBGM Advisor
✅ What gets exported: Players, Teams (with Seasons/Standings), GameAttributes, Draft Picks — everything the Advisor needs. No more manual checkbox clicking.
⚠️ Important: The bookmarklet only works on the FBGM page (play.zengm.com or play.football-gm.com). It only reads data — it does not modify your league.
